# Welcome to the Foslog Blog!

We're excited to announce the release of **Foslog v0.3.0**, which introduces our brand new blog feature! This update allows us to share news, insights, and stories about movies, series, games, books, and music with our community.

## What's New

### Blog System
- **Multilingual Support**: All blog posts are available in Catalan, Spanish, and English
- **Category Filtering**: Easily filter posts by topic (Releases, Films, Series, Games, Books, Music)
- **Clean Design**: A minimalist, functional design that keeps the focus on content
- **Markdown Support**: Full markdown rendering for rich, formatted content

## Why We Built This

We believe that sharing knowledge and experiences around media is essential. The blog allows us to:

- Announce new features and updates
- Share in-depth analyses of films, series, and games
- Discuss industry trends and news
- Provide tips for getting the most out of Foslog

## What's Next

This is just the beginning! We have exciting plans for future blog content, including:

- Deep dives into specific media titles
- Interviews with creators
- Community spotlights
- Tutorial series

Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you for being part of the Foslog community!
